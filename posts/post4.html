Saving the Tigers

Tigers are among the most iconic and majestic of the big cats, yet they are endangered due to poaching and habitat destruction. Conservation efforts have been instrumental in protecting them.

Tiger Populations

Once widespread across Asia, tiger populations have drastically decreased. Today, conservationists are working tirelessly to protect the few remaining wild tigers.

Conservation Success Stories

Despite the challenges, there have been successes in tiger conservation. Programs that protect tiger habitats and reduce poaching have helped increase the numbers of these magnificent animals in the wild.
